KEY FEATURES

For those not in the know, Minecraft is a Mojang project. In this game you have to fight on multiplayer servers, or build your own world on a local server. Also u can survive and kill ender dragon.

  • Creative

    In creative, you have access to all blocks, which opens up a huge fantasy of buildings to you. You also have access to all the armor in the game. You can share it with your friends.

  • Multiplayer Servers

    You can both survive in multiplayer as well as build your buildings with friends. Or find new ones.

  • Survival

    You can survive on your server alone or with friends. There are 5 difficulties. Peaceful, Easy, Medium, Hard, and Hardcore. On hardcore, you have 1 chance to die. After death, your world is removed.

  • Debug mode

    In this mode, you can see all the blocks in the game. Thanks to this, you can make your own resource pack! There is even a portal texture.

  • Spectator mode

    In this mode, you can fly through all the blocks in the world. Even a bedrock. You will be completely invisible and no one will see you.

  • Mobs

    In your journey you will mit defferent mobs, both peaceful and evil. From a little chicken to a creeper. From civillans to evil. Also, you can exp from killed mobs.
